HADES II by SuperGiant Games

A Button

Ways to Use the A Button

The blue icons on the screen that correspond to the A Button are called Action Icons. Various actions can be performed by simply pressing the A Button. The text displayed on the icon indicates the action Link can perform at that location. The text on the icon changes depending on where Link is standing. When nothing is displayed on the icon, it calls Navi.

Drop Down

When hanging from a wall or ledge, if "Drop" is displayed on the icon, you can let go.

Climb/Descend
Ladder

Push the 3D Stick toward the ladder to climb it. If "Drop" is displayed on the icon, you can jump down.

Climb
[Link near ladder image]

In the Direction You Want to Climb

For large blocks, push the 3D Stick while pressing the A Button. Depending on the location, you may be able to climb using only the 3D Stick.

Grab ➡ Push/Pull
A while grabbing or C
[Link pushing block image]

After grabbing a block (when "Grab" is displayed in front of the block, you can grab it with the A Button), push the 3D Stick in the direction you want to move while holding A.

Grab ➡ Throw / Put Down

When throwing a bomb, tilt the 3D Stick to build momentum before throwing. If you stand still, you'll place it at your feet.

A after grabbing + A
[Link throwing bomb image]

Items other than bombs can be thrown without tilting the 3D Stick.

[Treasure chest image]
Open

In addition to opening treasure chests, when you stand in front of a door, "Open" will be displayed on the icon. When talking to people, if the conversation continues, "Next" will be displayed. Please read the messages carefully before proceeding to the next message.

Other Action Icons
Talk

Stand in front of a person and "Talk" will be displayed on the icon (if you use "Z-Targeting," you can talk even from a distance).

Check

Displayed when reading signs, etc.
