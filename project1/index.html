Who Are You?

Melinoë's background

In Hades II, you play as Melinoë, daughter of Hades, on her quest to defeat Chronos, the Titan of Time. Death has little meaning to her, each time you fall, she will simply return to the Crossroads and start her adventure again. The further you progress towards your goal, the more you learn about Melinoë and her companions at the Crossroads.

Other Allies

You'll meet other allies in the Crossroads as well as on your journey down into the Underworld and up into Olympus.

Different Weapons Available

The different Nocturnal Arms available to you are the Witch's Staff, the Sister Blades, Umbral Flames, Moonstone Axe, and Argent Skull. Each weapon can be used in a different playstyle. The Sister Blades lean towards a more hack and slash approach while the Moonstone Axe lends to slow but devestating hits.

Animal Familiars

Throughout your runs, you will encounter different familiars you can lure to the Crossroads with treats to help you on your journey. Each will have its own unique traits and abilities to tailor to your preferences.

Magick Attacks
Holding X or B

Holding your attack or special button will unleash a magick attack, which creates special effects and higher damage at a cost of your mana meter. Be sure to manage using them in difficult encounters to unleash a mighty blow.

Arcana Cards

Unlock and equip different Arcana Cards inbetween runs to aid your journey. Each will have a different effect, such as a Death Defiance or a reroll reward.

Each arcana card can be upgraded, granting greater rewards. Each is themed after a character or encounter in game, such as The Wayward Son. This card grants you health upon entering a new room and is themed after the first game's main character, Zagreus.

Press R1 To use some Arcana effects

You will also tame wayword souls during your journey, where you can use the Psyche gained to upgrade your grasp. This will allow you to equip more Arcana cards, thus making your journey easier.

Infernal Troves

Infernal Troves can be activated after clearing a room. You will have a set time to kill enemies, where the quicker you defeat them the more coin you can earn to use at Charon's shop.

Boons

Throughout your journey, different gods and goddesses will offer you their boon. These boons will effect your attack, special, dash, and cast, granting damage boosts, elemental effects, or changing their funcionality entirely. Make sure to try every god's boon to see which matches your playstyle best!

Special Encounters
Battle Help

Occasionally, one of your allies may show up to aid you in clearing an encounter. Afterwards, you can talk to them and they should give you a boon!

Chance Meetings

You can run into other people along your journey instead of an encounter. They each offer different help, from a temporary armor to restoring your Death Defiances!
