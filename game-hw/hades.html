Hades

Publishing Info

  • Published by: Supergiant Games
  • Developed by: Supergiant Games
  • Released: December 6, 2018

Description

Hades is a rogue-like dungeon crawler in which you defy the god of death as you hack and slash your way out of the Underworld of Greek myth.

You, the player, control Zagreus, Prince of the Underworld. He is trying to escape the realm to get away from his father, Hades.

Zagreus is aided through his quest by other Olympians, granting him different boons and abilities each run.

Game Categories

  • Perspective Diagonal-Down
  • Gameplay Rougelike Action RPG
